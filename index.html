About the Perpetual Résumé

What it is

The Perpetual Résumé is a structured, long-term career archive — a single source of truth for your entire work history, from your first job to today. Instead of rewriting from scratch every time you need a résumé, you build the Perp once and generate tailored outputs from it.

It captures not just what you did, but how you did it and what it meant — through STAR stories, achievements, and role context that traditional résumés lose over time.

The three tiers

Free — Build Your Perp

Enter your complete career history: dates, companies, titles, responsibilities, achievements, and STAR stories. Export as structured JSON. View it as a formatted reverse-chronological résumé. No account, no server — your data stays on your device.

Subscription — AI-Tailored Résumés (coming soon)

Paste a job description and get a 2–3 page tailored résumé generated from your Perp. Options include age-agnostic formatting, chronological or functional layout, and JD-matched bullet selection. Your Perp is saved securely online.

Book — Free Year of Subscription (coming soon)

Readers of the Perpetual Résumé book receive one year of the subscription tier at no cost. Details coming as the book project develops.

The idea behind it

Most people can't remember what they did five jobs ago. The Perp solves that by making career documentation a continuous habit rather than a panic event. Write the entry while the memory is fresh. Forty years from now, it's all still there — searchable, structured, and ready to use.

It's not a résumé generator. It's a career operating system.
